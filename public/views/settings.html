Assume Depleting Principal

By default, Ballistic will assume that you expect to receive your goal income indefinitely. Selecting this option and entering your expected lifespan will cause it to instead assume that your investments will be expended when you reach the end of your estimated lifespan. This is a common feature in retirement plans based on tight budgets. Note: Depleting principal calculations are more complex, so estimates are currently restricted to within six months of the real date. Accuracy to within the month will be added once caching is implemented.

Include Inflation in Calculations

By default, Ballistic will not consider inflation. By enabling this option and entering an inflation rate, Ballistic will use the rate in it's calculations. It is generally a good idea to turn this feature on. It is not on by default due to the volatility of inflation rates between currencies and even within the same currency.
